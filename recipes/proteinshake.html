Strawberry Vanilla Protein Shake

Descriiption

Here is the recipe to make your very own Strawberry Vanilla Protein Shake!

Ingredient List

  • 12 oz of filtered water
  • 1 cup of filtered water ice
  • 2 cups of fresh strawberries
  • 2 servings of your favorite protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon of chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of other seeds

Steps

  1. Place ice in the blender
  2. Pour water in the blender
  3. Place the strawberries in the blender
  4. Pour the vanilla extract in
  5. Pour the seeds in
  6. Add the protein powder
  7. Blend until desired consistency
  8. Serve cold & refrigerate when finished
