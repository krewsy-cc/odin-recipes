Mens Coconut Coffee Scrub

Description

Today we will make a coffee sugar scrub that uses fresh ground cofee, orgranic cane sugar, ground cinnamon, and vanilla extract.

Ingredient List

  • Ground coffee
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Coarse Sugar
  • Coconut Oil
  • Essential Oil

Directions

  1. Grind coffee into a large bowl
  2. Mix sugar and coffee
  3. Mix in coconut oil, vanilla, and cinnamon
  4. Mix well
  5. Add essential oil
  6. Store in glass mason jar in a dry, cool area
